{\setstretch{0.9} \small \noindent {\it  Note on the Italian grading system}. Students are graded after each class  on a scale ranging from 0 to 30 with 18 as passing mark. Weights (CFU) are assigned to such individual grades to compute the average class grade. Degrees are awarded after successfully defending a research thesis. Final degree grades range from 0 to 110 with 66 as passing mark. {``Cum laude"} mentions may be added to the highest grades as special distinctions.
